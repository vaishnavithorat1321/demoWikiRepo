How to divide two numbers

This topic demostrates the division of two numbers

Calculator app is installed and functional.

You know the numbers you want to divide. (Note: second number must not be zero)

  1. Enter the numerator (first number).
  2. Click the รท (division) button.
  3. Enter the denominator (second number).
  4. Click the = button to get the result.

The calculator displays the quotient of the division.

Input: 50 รท 10

Output: 5
